Featured Dance

Pantsula Dance

Pantsula dance is a highly energetic and dynamic South African dance style that originated in the townships during the apartheid era. It combines rapid, precise footwork, synchronized movements, and exaggerated gestures, often performed in groups to showcase unity and rhythm.
